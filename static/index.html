Welcome to the Furry Digital Archive

Who are we

Welcome to Furry Digital Archive! We are a community-driven platform aiming to preserve the vibrant digital content of the furry fandom. With a window of opportunity currently open while all our community members are still active and able to contribute, we believe now is the time to safeguard the cultural heritage of our fandom.

We aim to secure and archive the correspondences of the organisers who set up the furry conventions, with a keen interest in understanding the difference between a healthy convention and an unhealthy one. It's essential to capture this knowledge before it's lost to time, as it will prove invaluable for future generations in creating and managing their events.

Our overarching goal is to document how the furry community has grown and changed over the years, capturing our collective story as we evolve.

This initiative is a direct response to a call to action made in a talk by Sturmovik at the 2023 Anthrocon. The talk, named "Historic Preservation of the Furry Fandom", argued for the creation of such a community. This webpage, brought to life by Leon The Stag, serves as a launching point for creating a digital archive for the furry fandom.

How to Contribute

If you're interested in contributing to the Furry Digital Archive, we're thrilled to have you! Discussions about our initiative are currently taking place in the "Furry Preservation" Telegram group, serving as our primary communication channel until we form a proper organization.

We're especially interested in backing up the following types of digital content:

  • Furry webcomics and websites
  • Pictures from various conventions like Anthrocon and Midwest Furfest, among others
  • Furry art and digital literature
  • Archives of furry online games and virtual spaces

These should be made available as a completed archive, and the torrent file and magnet link can be posted on the Telegram.

If you're a frontend developer and want to help improve our website, you're welcome to contribute to our Github page. Your potential improvements will be greatly appreciated!

Preserving our furry digital heritage is a communal effort, and every contribution, no matter how small, gets us closer to our goal. Join us in preserving our fandom's history!
